Introduction

Raj Srivastava

Frontend Developer and Visual Interaction Designer based in Varanasi, India. I craft immersive digital environments with modern JavaScript, 3D graphics, and scroll-driven designs.

Mindset

Minimal, Not Empty

I believe the simplest designs require the most discipline. Every interface, every animation, every delay is intentional, trimmed to reveal only what matters. Not to impress, but to express. Not to overwhelm, but to whisper with elegance.

Tech as Canvas

Code that Breathes

Whether it's scroll-controlled flythroughs in Three.js, or animated Zen browser extensions built in WebAssembly, I view code as a medium, not just to function, but to feel.

Philosophy

Interaction is Emotion

I don't add buttons where gestures can do. I don't design for attention. I design for presence. I aim to make interfaces that listen, not just shout.

Current Work

Real Projects, Real Impact

I'm building a Mental Health Companion App with breathing tools, journaling. I’ve developed a stock market simulator using Uniswap’s pricing model to teach market dynamics through supply-demand logic. Every project reflects a personal itch to fix, to understand, to reimagine.
